Evaluation and evaluation criteria
Theory   50%:

- Written exam, multiple choice + open questions during exam period (June)
- Lab   50%:

	- Permanent evaluation
	- Lab assignments
	- Test subnetting
	- Theory exercise

The assessment activity for the individual course components (theory and laboratory) assesses individual learning objectives. Theory assesses learning objective 1, while the lab assesses the other learning objectives. If the student scores less than 6/20 for one of the teaching and learning activities (theory or lab), the total for the entire course can be a maximum of 9/20.
